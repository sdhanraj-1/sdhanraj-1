ResourcePage

There are already a number of initiatives underway. This page introduces resources to explore to understand what is currently being done to address the accumulation of plastics in our oceans.

International Coastal Cleanup

The International Coastal Cleanup (ICC) engages volunteers to removed trash from beaches and waterways around the world. Volunteering your time can have a small impact. ICC provides a clear way for potential volunteers to learn about the problem and become part of the solution in a hands-on fashion.

5Gyres

The 5Gyres is a non profit dedicated to Science Advocacy and Education. There current research includes years of work behind pollution monitoring and plastics alternatives. 5 Gyres also plays a leadership role in advocating for better policies with notable work in California. 5Gyres also has a virtual web series called our Trash Academy on Youtube to advocate for the masses

The Ocean Cleanup

The Ocean Cleanup deploying solutions for for removing plastics from the oceans. They also have a fleet of interceptors to capture plastics in highly polluted rivers before they can get to the gyres. The Ocean Cleanup is currently experiementing with the third iteration of their clearing Interceptor nicknamed "System 003".
